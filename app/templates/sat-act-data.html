SAT & ACT data

Below you will find concept categorizations for questions from selected official SATs and ACTs. To see the full dataset including recently administered tests, please request access to the Google Sheet linked below.

SAT test codes in the table are in YYMM format of the date the test was administered, while ACT test codes are in YYYYMM format. (For example, 2203 is the March 2022 SAT, while 202204 is the April 2022 ACT.) SATs 1601-1610 are practice tests #1-10 from the College Board . Many official tests are available courtesy of Reddit's SAT and ACT communities.
