SAT & ACT data

Below you will find data for many official SATs and ACTs that can assist your test prep endeavors. You can access PDFs of official SATs here and official ACTs here .

SAT test codes in the table are in YYMM format of the date the test was administered, while ACT test codes are in YYYYMM format. (For example, 1810 is the Oct 2018 SAT, while 202012 is the Dec 2020 ACT.) SATs 1601-1610 are practice tests #1-10 from the College Board .
