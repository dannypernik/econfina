Econfina Creek Canoe Rentals F Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation by Customers

    1.1 Notice Period for Cancellation. To receive a full refund on your reservation deposit, cancellations must be made at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled reservation time. Cancellations made within this period will be processed promptly, and you will receive your refund within 7-10 business days.

    1.2 How to Cancel. Cancellations can be made by contacting our customer support team via:

    • Email: econfinarentals@gmail.com
    • Phone: 850-601-0157

    Please provide your reservation details, including your name, reservation date and time, and any confirmation number received at the time of booking.

    1.3 Late Cancellations. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the reservation time will not be eligible for a refund of the deposit. We understand that emergencies happen, and we encourage you to contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel within this period. While we cannot guarantee a refund, we will consider each situation on a case-by-case basis.

2. Cancellation by Econfina Creek Canoe Rentals LLC

    2.1 Weather-Related Cancellations. In the event that we need to close due to poor weather conditions, we will notify you as soon as possible. All reservations affected by weather-related closures will receive a full refund of their deposit. We prioritize the safety and well-being of our customers and staff, and appreciate your understanding in these circumstances.

    2.2 Other Unforeseen Closures. Should we need to close for any other unforeseen reasons, such as maintenance issues or emergencies, we will also provide a full refund for all affected reservations. You will be notified immediately, and we will assist in rescheduling your reservation if desired.

3. Refund Process

    Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at the time of booking. Once a refund is processed, please allow 7-10 business days for the funds to appear in your account. If you have not received your refund within this period, please contact our customer support team for assistance.

4. Contact Information

    For any questions or concerns regarding our cancellation policy, please reach out to our customer support team:

    • Email: econfinarentals@gmail.com
    • Phone: 850-601-0157

Thank you for choosing Econfina Creek Canoe Rentals LLC . We value your business and strive to provide the best possible experience for our customers.
